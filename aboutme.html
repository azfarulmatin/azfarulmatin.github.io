About Me

Hi! My name is Azfarul Matin and I’m a self-taught photographer/videographer based in Singapore. I am currently an undergraduate studying Computer Engineering at the National University of Singapore (NUS).

I developed an interest in photography after wanting to capture the moments that matter. To me, I find it important that I take photographs that I can strongly connect and resonate with. While photography is no doubt the love of my life, travelling is also something that I truly enjoy. I’m endlessly inspired by nature and the sense of adventure I get when exploring new places. I love making friends with people, waking up to new horizons and discovering new things in life.

In addition to photography, I have a passion for videography. I enjoy creating compelling visual stories through video, capturing both the big moments and the subtle details. From interviews and documentaries to event coverage and creative projects, videography allows me to explore a different dimension of visual storytelling. I love how I am able to tell a story and showcase my creative side through my work.

Think we could create something together? Feel free to drop me an email!
